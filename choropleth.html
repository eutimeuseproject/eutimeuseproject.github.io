Insights & Observations

  • Differences between gender Men spend more time in work and leisure than women. However women are more interested by household and personal care. This differences are very small and lead us to say that there is not a big difference in the way that men and women spend their time

  • Differences between countries United Kingdom works more than France and Germany but has more time leisure than France We have little observations so it's probably not the ground truth. With many samples this analysis could help us to explain some economic differences between these countries
